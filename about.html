About Me

I’m Muhammad Waleed, a dedicated GIS Analyst with a passion for turning spatial data into actionable insight. Over the past few years I’ve:

  • Processed large satellite and drone imagery datasets for land‑use mapping.
  • Built end‑to‑end ArcGIS/QGIS workflows for utilities, urban planning and environmental projects.
  • Developed web maps and dashboards (Leaflet, Google Maps & Mapbox) to help non‑technical stakeholders grasp complex geospatial patterns at a glance.

My toolkit spans Python (GeoPandas, Rasterio, ArcPy), SQL/PostGIS, and JavaScript for front‑end visualization. I’m fascinated by how location intelligence can drive better policy and business decisions—whether it’s optimising road networks or identifying climate‑resilient sites.

Outside of work you’ll likely find me tinkering with open‑source geospatial projects, contributing to community mapping initiatives, or hiking with a GPS logger in hand.
