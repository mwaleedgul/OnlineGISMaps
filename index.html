Portfolio Notes

This portfolio demonstrates the practical application of Geographic Information Systems (GIS) in cadastral mapping, land information systems, and spatial data management. The work presented reflects real-world workflows, including parcel digitization, georeferencing, topology correction, and integration of land record attributes.

The interactive map component showcases multiple GeoJSON layers representing cadastral datasets. Each layer can be independently controlled, enabling visualization and analysis of land parcels, boundaries, and survey-related features. Attribute information is dynamically linked and displayed through feature popups, supporting efficient interpretation and validation of spatial data.

All datasets are structured to reflect real land administration scenarios, where spatial accuracy, data consistency, and usability are critical. The system is developed using open-source web GIS technologies, ensuring accessibility, flexibility, and scalability without reliance on proprietary platforms.

Key highlights of this portfolio include:

• Accurate representation of cadastral boundaries
• Structured spatial data management using GeoJSON formats
• Integration of spatial and attribute data for decision support
• Scalable architecture suitable for land information and planning systems

This implementation reflects the capability to design and deliver GIS solutions that align with practical requirements in land management, urban planning, and administrative workflows.

Detailed project datasets and complete GIS workflows can be demonstrated upon request.
